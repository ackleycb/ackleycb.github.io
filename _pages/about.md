Welcome to my website! I am a Ph.D. research economist and data scientist. I specialize in analyzing large health claims databases from Medicare, Medicaid, and commercial insurers to study issues related to health care costs, quality, and utilization.

Some of my recent research projects include developing regional price indices for medical services, examining how insurance design features like tiered networks affect patient choices and negotiated prices, and studying whether reimbursement gaps between Medicaid and Medicare affect physician supply and patient care.

On this webpage, you can find links to my research papers, online data analysis textbook, associated software, and past teaching materials.
